Itinerary

What to expect

←→

Pass by: Bali 4U Tours

This tour offers the most recommended places in Ubud pack in much of the area’s main highlight attractions in a single excursion. Your day begins with private hotel pick up at 08.30 am (lobby hotel) and be ready to see: Batuan Temple, a village temple decorated beautifully with sculptures and other types of ancient stone. Tegenungan Waterfall, the falls where you can enjoy the impressively green valley panorama with cascading water as a centerpiece. Coffee Plantation, experience a coffee tour and try local coffee and tea. Tegalalang Rice Terrace, exotic rice fields located in a beautiful valley. Sacred Ubud Monkey Forest, the natural habitat of Balinese long tailed Monkey (macaca fascicularis). In addition, you can have 1-hour free time to explore center Ubud such as: Ubud Palace and Market.
